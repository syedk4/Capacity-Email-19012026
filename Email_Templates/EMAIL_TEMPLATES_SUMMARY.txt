QUICK START:
================================================================================

1. Generate your report:
   py sprint_capacity_app.py --analyze

2. Open email_template_simple.html in a text editor

3. Replace placeholders (use Sprint 2 and Sprint 3 data):
   [DATE] → 2026-01-13 14:41:12
   [TOTAL_MEMBERS] → 7
   [SPRINT2_PERIOD] → 2026-01-14 to 2026-01-27
   [SPRINT2_WORKING_DAYS] → 8
   [SPRINT2_CAPACITY] → 83.9
   [SPRINT3_PERIOD] → 2026-01-28 to 2026-02-10
   [SPRINT3_WORKING_DAYS] → 10
   [SPRINT3_CAPACITY] → 100

4. Update team member rows with actual data from Sprint 2 and Sprint 3

5. Open in browser to preview

6. Copy and paste into your email client

7. Send!
