‚ö†Ô∏è KEY POINTS TO REMEMBER:
================================================================================

1. Send on the LAST DAY of the current sprint
   Example: If Sprint 1 ends on Jan 13, send on Jan 13

2. Show the NEXT 2 UPCOMING SPRINTS (not the current sprint)
   Example: If today is last day of Sprint 1, show Sprint 2 and Sprint 3

3. DON'T include the current sprint in the email
   Example: Don't show Sprint 1 if you're sending on the last day of Sprint 1

4. Use Sprint 2 and Sprint 3 data from your generated report
